Collector Resources

Everything about how I care for, pack, and ship my cards.

Card Care

Every card is handled carefully and kept in a protective sleeve. Higher value cards go straight into a toploader or a one-touch magnetic holder. I never touch card faces with bare hands, and cards are stored away from sunlight and moisture so they stay in the condition you see.

Shipping

Orders ship the next business day. Every card is sleeved, top loaded, and packed with care inside a protective mailer so it arrives exactly as shown. Tracking is included on every order. Every package also comes with a handwritten thank you note from me.

FAQ

Are the cards authentic?
Yes. Every card is a real card from my own collection, hand checked by me. If anything is ever not as described, you get your money back.

How do I pay?
Checkout is through PayPal, so your payment is secure.

Can I trade instead of buy?
Yes! Cards marked "For Trade" are open to offers. Use the Propose a Trade button on the card, or Contact me.

What is the Mr PokeCards grade?
It's my own honest assessment of a card's condition, not a professional grading service. I tell you exactly what I see.

What does the QR code on my card do?
Scan it to open that card's page any time, with its photos, details, and unique ID.
